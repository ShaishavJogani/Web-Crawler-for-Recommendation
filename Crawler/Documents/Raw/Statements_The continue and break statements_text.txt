
At times, you would like to re-iterate a loop without executing the remaining statement within the loop. Thecontinuestatement causes the loop to re-iterate and start over from the top most statement inside
the loop.
Where there is an ability to re-iterate the loop, there is an ability to exit the loop when required. At any given
moment, if you'd like to exit a loop and end all further work within the loop, thebreakought to be
used.
