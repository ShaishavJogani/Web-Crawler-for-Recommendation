
privateis a Javakeywordwhich declares a member'saccessas private. That is, the member is only visible within the class,
not from any other class (including subclasses). The visibility ofprivatemembers extends tonested classes.
Please note: Because access modifiers are not handled at instance level but at class level, private members of an object are visible from other instances of the same class!
Syntax:

privatevoidmethod();
